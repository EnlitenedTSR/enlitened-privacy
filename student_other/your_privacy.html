Your Privacy

We take your privacy really seriously. We’ll only ever use your data in the way we tell you.

You'll always be in control. You can ask to see a copy of your data at any time, and if you want to delete the app, we will make sure all of your personally identifiable information is totally removed.

Your university will not see your individual data or be able to connect it with your personal details. Our systems only enable universities to see data and trends from groups of more than five students. You can submit ideas anonymously if you prefer.

For the app to be personalised, and for your university to be able to see analysis on the wellbeing and engagement of groups of students so they can know where and how to help, we need your consent to:

  • Enlitened receiving your information from your university records systems. i.e. enrollment data about which course you’re on and what type of student you are; demographic data such as where you’re from, your gender, ethnicity and disabilities.
  • Enlitened and your university to be able to use, analyse and process that data, together with the data you provide as you use the app.
